About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student working in cryptography at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington. I am co-advised by Stefano Tessaro and Rachel Lin.

I am interested in cryptography and also in related fields of theoretical computater science. I love solving concrete, discrete problems. My currently focus is on various signature schemes, such as blind signatures and threshold signatures.
